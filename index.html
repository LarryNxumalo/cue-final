WHo We Are

Cue Consulting and Advisory is a proudly 100% black-owned and managed company offering a range of specialised end to end information technology services and solutions. Our company is provisioned to work with entities in the public and private sector. We are committed to servicing a broad range of industries, and pride ourselves on understanding and meeting our clients' needs effectively and efficiently. From management and business analysis, application development and more, our service offering and solutions are designed to positively impact the businesses and organisations that entrust their IT requirements to us.

Vision

We strive to provide clients with superior information technology services by designing and implementing practical IT solutions that cater for our clients’ diverse needs.

Mission

We endeavour to reach our vision by using our expertise and experience to provide digital solutions, products and services that contribute to the realisation of our clients’ goals and success.
